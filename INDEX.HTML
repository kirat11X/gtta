About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2021, Glide Table Tennis Academy (GTTA) has established itself as a premier training facility for table tennis enthusiasts and competitive players alike. Our mission is to develop players who excel not only in technique but also in sportsmanship and mental fortitude.

We envision a future where our players represent their countries at international competitions, bringing glory to themselves and their communities through the sport they love.

Our state-of-the-art facility features 8 professional tables, robotic training systems, video analysis technology, and a dedicated fitness area designed specifically for table tennis athletes.

Training Programs

Beginner Training

Learn the fundamentals of grip, stance, and basic strokes. Develop coordination and ball control through structured drills designed for new players.

Advanced & Elite Coaching

Personalized training programs for competitive players focusing on technical refinement, tactical development, and match preparation.

Fitness & Mental Conditioning

Specialized physical training and mental resilience programs to enhance performance under pressure and prevent injuries.

Match Play & Tournament Exposure

Regular competitive sessions and participation in local, state, and national tournaments with coaching support.

Intensive Camps

Week-long training camps during school breaks featuring guest coaches, specialized workshops, and competitive match play.

Training Methodology

Physical Approach

Our training emphasizes proper biomechanics and efficient movement patterns. Players develop explosive footwork, core stability, and stroke consistency through progressive drills that build muscle memory and coordination.

Technological Approach

We integrate cutting-edge technology into our training programs, including high-speed video analysis, ball trajectory tracking systems, and robotic trainers that simulate match situations with precision.

Success Pathway

Our systematic approach ensures comprehensive player development:

Technique
